TiGrESS...

TiGrESS is a smart system for managing career development and employment activities. It is designed to accomplish the national objective to improve graduate employment rate nationwide.

Institutions and employers are just a click away to connect with each other, forming a connection for further employment activities. With TiGrESS, undergraduates and graduates are able to interact with the institutions and employers, view upcoming events, apply for internships and jobs, participate in surveys and profiling tests, and so on.

Employers

TiGrESS gives the employer tools to communicate with students, post internships and graduate jobs and co-ordinate activities with Career Centres.

Students

Undergraduate and graduates are able to interact with the institutions and employers, view upcoming events, apply for internships and jobs, participate in surveys and profiling tests, and so on.

Career Centre

Career Centres can now manage careers service processes more efficiently and connect with more employers in the country.
